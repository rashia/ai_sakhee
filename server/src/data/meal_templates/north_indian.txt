## NOTES FOR PCOS MANAGEMENT

### BEST CHOICES FOR PCOS:
1. **Low GI Millets**: Bajra, jowar, ragi (mandua), barnyard millet (jhangora)
2. **Protein-Rich Legumes**: Horse gram (gahat), black gram, sattu, all lentils
3. **Beneficial Vegetables**: Bitter gourd, bottle gourd, okra, spinach, fenugreek
4. **Healthy Fats**: Ghee in moderation, mustard oil, nuts, seeds
5. **Probiotic Foods**: Yogurt, buttermilk, fermented preparations

### PORTION CONTROL:
- Breakfast: 300-400 calories
- Lunch: 400-500 calories
- Dinner: 300-400 calories
- Snacks: 100-150 calories (2 per day)

### COOKING METHODS:
- Prefer: Steaming, grilling, baking, roasting
- Minimize: Deep frying, excessive oil use
- Use: Non-stick cookware, air fryer when possible

### MEAL TIMING:
- Breakfast: Within 1 hour of waking
- Lunch: 12-2 PM
- Dinner: Before 8 PM (3 hours before sleep)
- Snacks: Mid-morning and evening

### HYDRATION:
- 8-10 glasses of water daily
- Herbal teas, buttermilk, lemon water
- Avoid sugary drinks and excessive caffeine

### REGIONAL ADAPTATIONS:
- All meals can be customized based on:
  * Available ingredients in your region
  * Family cooking traditions
  * Personal taste preferences
  * Budget constraints
  * Seasonal availability

### MEDICAL DISCLAIMER:
These meal plans are general guidelines for PCOS management. Always consult with:
- Registered dietitian for personalized meal plans
- Endocrinologist for medical management
- Healthcare provider before making major dietary changes

### PORTION SIZES:

## RECIPE CUSTOMIZATION TIPS

### For Weight Management:
- Reduce rice/roti portions by 20-30%
- Increase vegetable portions
- Choose grilled/baked over fried
- Use minimal oil (1-2 tsp per meal)

### For Insulin Resistance:
- Focus on Low GI options (⭐⭐⭐)
- Pair carbs with protein
- Include fiber-rich vegetables
- Choose whole grains over refined

### For Hormonal Balance:
- Include omega-3 rich foods (fish, walnuts)
- Anti-inflammatory spices (turmeric, ginger)
- Cruciferous vegetables (cauliflower, cabbage)
- Adequate protein at each meal

### For Budget-Conscious Choices:
- Dal-based meals are economical
- Seasonal vegetables are cheaper
- Eggs are affordable protein
- Bulk-cook grains and freeze

### For Time-Saving:
- Batch cook dal and freeze
- Pre-chop vegetables
- Use pressure cooker for faster cooking
- Keep healthy snacks ready
