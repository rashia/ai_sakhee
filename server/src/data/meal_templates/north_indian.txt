# North Indian PCOS-Friendly Meals

## Breakfast Options

### Besan Chilla (Low GI: ★★★)
- Ingredients: Besan (chickpea flour) 100g, onion 1 small, tomato 1, coriander, turmeric, cumin
- Macros: Protein 15g, Carbs 20g, Fats 5g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 10 mins
- Tip: Add flaxseeds for omega-3 boost

### Moong Dal Khichdi
- Ingredients: Moong dal 50g, brown rice 30g, vegetables, ghee 1 tsp
- Macros: Protein 12g, Carbs 35g, Fats 6g
- Budget: ₹35-40
- Prep: 25 mins
- Tip: Use brown rice for lower GI

### Vegetable Poha (Low GI)
- Ingredients: Flattened rice 50g, mixed vegetables, peanuts, curry leaves
- Macros: Protein 8g, Carbs 30g, Fats 7g
- Budget: ₹20-25
- Prep: 15 mins
- Tip: Soak poha briefly to reduce carb content

## Lunch Options

### Palak Paneer with Roti
- Ingredients: Spinach 200g, paneer 100g, whole wheat roti 2, spices
- Macros: Protein 20g, Carbs 40g, Fats 15g
- Budget: ₹60-70
- Prep: 30 mins
- Tip: Use low-fat paneer for better health

### Rajma Curry with Brown Rice
- Ingredients: Rajma 100g (soaked), brown rice 50g, onion, tomato, spices
- Macros: Protein 18g, Carbs 45g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹40-50
- Prep: 40 mins (after soaking)
- Tip: Rajma is rich in fiber and protein

### Mixed Vegetable Sabzi with Multigrain Roti
- Ingredients: Cauliflower, beans, carrots, peas, multigrain flour
- Macros: Protein 10g, Carbs 35g, Fats 6g
- Budget: ₹45-55
- Prep: 25 mins
- Tip: Add bitter gourd for insulin sensitivity

## Dinner Options

### Quinoa Pulao
- Ingredients: Quinoa 60g, mixed vegetables, ghee 1 tsp
- Macros: Protein 12g, Carbs 30g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹70-80
- Prep: 20 mins
- Tip: Quinoa is complete protein with low GI

### Dal Tadka with Cucumber Salad
- Ingredients: Masoor dal 70g, cucumber 100g, tomatoes, spices
- Macros: Protein 16g, Carbs 28g, Fats 5g
- Budget: ₹30-40
- Prep: 30 mins
- Tip: Light dinner aids better sleep

### Vegetable Khichdi
- Ingredients: Moong dal 50g, rice 30g, vegetables, ghee

- Budget: ₹35-45
- Prep: 25 mins
- Tip: Easy to digest, perfect for dinner
