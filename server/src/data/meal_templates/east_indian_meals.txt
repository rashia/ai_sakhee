## WEST BENGAL REGIONAL CUISINE
*West Bengal’s cuisine balances rice, fish, and mustard-based gravies with light vegetable preparations. For PCOS-friendly adaptation, mustard oil is used sparingly, with more steaming, grilling, and brown rice substitutions to lower glycemic load.*
