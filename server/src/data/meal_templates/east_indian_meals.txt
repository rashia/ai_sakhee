# East Indian PCOS-Friendly Meals

## Breakfast Options

### Moong Dal Cheela (Bengal Style)
- Ingredients: Moong dal 100g, ginger, green chili, onion, nigella seeds
- Macros: Protein 16g, Carbs 22g, Fats 5g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 15 mins
- Tip: Soak dal overnight for better digestion

### Muri (Puffed Rice) with Vegetables
- Ingredients: Puffed rice 40g, potato 50g, peanuts 20g, mustard oil, green chili
- Macros: Protein 7g, Carbs 32g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹15-20
- Prep: 10 mins
- Tip: Light breakfast, use minimal potato

### Chirer Pulao (Flattened Rice)
- Ingredients: Chire 50g, peas 30g, potato 30g, peanuts, ghee 1 tsp
- Macros: Protein 8g, Carbs 30g, Fats 7g
- Budget: ₹20-25
- Prep: 15 mins
- Tip: Rinse chire well to reduce carbs

### Daliya (Broken Wheat Porridge)
- Ingredients: Broken wheat 50g, milk 100ml, jaggery 10g, nuts 10g
- Macros: Protein 10g, Carbs 35g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 20 mins
- Tip: Use minimal jaggery or stevia

### Litti (Baked Version) - Bihar
- Ingredients: Whole wheat flour 80g, sattu 40g, minimal ghee
- Macros: Protein 12g, Carbs 45g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹20-25
- Prep: 35 mins
- Tip: Bake instead of roasting in ghee

### Jolpan (Assamese Breakfast)
- Ingredients: Bora saul (sticky rice) 40g, curd 100g, jaggery 10g
- Macros: Protein 6g, Carbs 35g, Fats 4g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 15 mins
- Tip: Use small portion of rice
