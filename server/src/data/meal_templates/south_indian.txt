# South Indian PCOS-Friendly Meals

## Breakfast Options

### Ragi Dosa (Low GI: ★★★)
- Ingredients: Ragi flour 80g, urad dal 20g, fenugreek seeds
- Macros: Protein 10g, Carbs 28g, Fats 4g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 20 mins (batter fermented overnight)
- Tip: Ragi is excellent for PCOS management

### Oats Idli
- Ingredients: Oats 60g, curd 50ml, rava 20g, vegetables
- Macros: Protein 12g, Carbs 30g, Fats 5g
- Budget: ₹30-35
- Prep: 25 mins
- Tip: High fiber, low GI breakfast option

### Pesarattu (Green Gram Dosa)
- Ingredients: Moong dal 100g, rice 20g, ginger, green chilies
- Macros: Protein 15g, Carbs 25g, Fats 3g
- Budget: ₹25-30
- Prep: 15 mins (after soaking)
- Tip: Rich in protein, excellent for insulin resistance

## Lunch Options

### Sambar Rice with Vegetable Curry
- Ingredients: Brown rice 60g, sambar vegetables, tamarind, spices
- Macros: Protein 14g, Carbs 45g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹40-50
- Prep: 35 mins
- Tip: Complete meal with vegetables and lentils

### Bisi Bele Bath (with millets)
- Ingredients: Foxtail millet 50g, toor dal 30g, vegetables
- Macros: Protein 12g, Carbs 38g, Fats 7g
- Budget: ₹45-55
- Prep: 40 mins
- Tip: Comfort food, PCOS-adapted with millets

### Avial with Brown Rice
- Ingredients: Mixed vegetables in coconut, brown rice, curd
- Macros: Protein 10g, Carbs 40g, Fats 12g
- Budget: ₹50-60
- Prep: 30 mins
- Tip: High in fiber and healthy fats

## Dinner Options

### Quinoa Pongal
- Ingredients: Quinoa 60g, moong dal 30g, pepper, cumin, ghee
- Macros: Protein 14g, Carbs 32g, Fats 8g
- Budget: ₹70-80
- Prep: 25 mins
- Tip: Protein-rich, low GI dinner

### Ragi Mudde with Greens Curry
- Ingredients: Ragi flour 80g, leafy greens, coconut
- Macros: Protein 9g, Carbs 35g, Fats 10g
- Budget: ₹35-45
- Prep: 20 mins
- Tip: Traditional, nutrient-dense meal

### Vegetable Upma (with vegetables)
- Ingredients: Rava 50g, vegetables 100g, peanuts, spices
- Macros: Protein 8g, Carbs 30g, Fats 7g
- Budget: ₹25-35
- Prep: 15 mins
- Tip: Light and easy to digest
